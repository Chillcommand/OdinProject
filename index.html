Some recipes to help conquering France.

Bouchée à la reine:

For the vol au vent pastry cases:

  • 500g puff pastry
  • 1 egg yolk (glaze)

For the garnish:

  • 500g veal sweetbread, raw
  • 2 chicken fillets or chicken quenelles (optional)
  • 250g button mushrooms
  • 2 litres chicken stock
  • 30 g de beurre
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Espelette pepper
  • Fresh black truffle

For the sauce suprême:

  • 130g unsalted butter (90 + 40g)
  • 90g flour
  • 1.5 litres chicken stock
  • 30g crème fraîche
  • Splash of Madeira wine (1 bottle cap)
  • 2 egg yolks
